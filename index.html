PiMini is the World's first-ever project made to combine education platforms with the crypto world.

PiMini is a governance token of PiMini project. The motive of the PiMini project is to bring out the adoption of cryptocurrency by education platforms to endure the Crypto use in a day to day life and it also provides an opportunity to become a professional developer in different niches like Frontend Developer, Backend Developer, Full stack Developer, Mobile Developer, Game Developer, Data Scientist Developer, Software Developer, NFT Artist.

PiMini is going to have its own educational website, application and a real life university. This is going to serve the online and offline teaching of competitive exams and crypto education world wide.

There will be two options to get enrolled. First is to opt for the online education where you can get access to your chosen courses by sitting in any part of the world with the help of our crypto integrated educational website and platforms .

The second way of learning is to join the PiMini university and learn by attending the classes physically in completely different university environment where you have a real life experience of using your PiMini tokens within the university for different aspects.

PiMini Plans

PiMini Plans

PiMini is basically working on online and offline aspects. The online aspects cosist of educational website and application that is integrated with crypto wallet. The website and application will run through three tests before releasing the final version.

Alfa Testing

The first test is Alpha testing. Alpha testing is a type of software testing performed to identify bugs before releasing the product to real users or to the public. Alpha Testing is one of the user acceptance testing. It is performed at developer's site.

Beta Testing

The second test is Beta testing. Beta testing provides insights into product functionality, and also helps you better understand user experience. Going beyond lab performance tests, beta testing reveals whether or not the same level of performance is achievable in actual user environments.

Release Candidate Testing

The third test is Release candidiate. This is the stage of product stabilization, all product features have been designed, coded and tested through one or more beta cycles with no known showstopper-class bugs. A release is called code complete when the development team agrees that no entirely new source code will be added to this release.

Final Stage

The next is Final stage. In final stage software product is released and is made available in the market for selling and purchasing of services, after completing all marketing formalities and commercialization activities, which may include security and compliance testing, along with the nationwide or the worldwide availability of the product.

PiMini NFT

PiMini NFT

PiMini NFTs provide you actual utility where you can use these NFTs for a specified number of times to avail discount on purchases. The special algorithm will keep a track of number of times an NFT has been used for discounts. Once the usage limit comes to zero you can refill it with PiMini tokens. More the number of times an NFT exceeds its usage limit and get refilled, more rare and valuable it becomes.

Elite Book

This NFT can be used 100 times to avail 30% discount on each time you buy a course or product from online or from the university. There will be 100 Elite book NFTs.

Library

This NFT gives you a title of Library Ambassador. You can use this NFT 75 times to avail 20% discount on every purchase you make on PiMini online/offline platform. There will be 100 library NFTs.

Hostel Room

This NFT gives you a title of Hostel Ambassador. You can use this NFT 50 times to avail 10% discount on every purchase you make on PiMini online/offline platform. There will be 100 hostel room NFTs.

King NFT

This NFT is the king of all other NFTs of PiMini which represents the whole University. There will be only 1 copy of this NFT and the person who owns this NFT will be called as the PiMini King/Queen. King NFT can be used unlimited times to avail 50% discount on every purchase you make.

The specialty of king NFT lies in its royalty. You will get -

0.5% of the trading volume.
1% monthly shares from the online purchases made by users.
1% monthly shares from the offline purchases made by users.
